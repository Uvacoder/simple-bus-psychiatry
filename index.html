My Philosophy

My life’s purpose is to diminish the amount of suffering in the world, by way of helping one person at a time.

It is my belief that, when introduced to the right environment, an individual can make great progress towards achieving optimal mental health. I utilize a holistic approach that combines psychotherapy and pharmacotherapy. The value of finding the right balance of medication should not be underestimated.

Working in tandem, we can enhance your overall outlook on life by focusing on the areas that are causing you distress. It is important to me that we collaborate in a way that is consistent with your values and beliefs.

You may feel lost, but you are worthy. My goal is to assist you in finding your purpose; providing the environment necessary for you to prevail.

— Dr. Pete Bowman, MD

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do we convene during the COVID pandemic?

  • For the foreseeable future, appointments will be held over secure video chat. I use the service provided by WeCounsel.

  • What is the cost?

  • Do you accept insurance?
  • No — I am what is termed an “out-of-network provider.” This means that I can provide you with a superbill for submission to your insurance. You may be reimbursed for a portion of, or for the entire cost of each visit.
  • I advise that you contact your insurance company to determine your out-of-network benefits and to acquire a pre-authorization, prior to scheduling an appointment at Prevail Psychiatry.
  • What forms of payment do you accept?
  • Prevail Psychiatry accepts credit/debit, cash, check, or Health Savings Account (HSA) payments.
  • How often will we meet?
  • Each individual is unique, and therefore, appointment frequency is determined on a case-by-case basis.
  • What is your cancellation policy?
  • Appointments rescheduled or cancelled with more than 24 hours notice will not be subject to a fee. Those rescheduled or cancelled within 24 hours of the appointment time will be assessed the full fee.
